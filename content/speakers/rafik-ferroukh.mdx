Rafik Ferroukh is a Solutions Architect with a passion for simplifying technology and over 20 years of experience in the IT sector.

Currently at Harvest Groupe, he strives to make IT accessible to everyone by advocating a pragmatic, no-nonsense approach. A Java / API / AI / Cloud specialist, Rafik is a fervent advocate of “No Bullshit” and a popular speaker known for his ability to explain complex subjects in simple terms.

Rafik is also an Enablers Enthusiast, always seeking solutions that simplify and improve the daily work of both technical and business teams.

Outside the tech world, Rafik nurtures two other passions:

    Tech community engagement — hosting his YouTube channel: @DeBuzzTech
    Cooking — sharing his creations on Instagram: @raf_cooking

Based in Paris, Rafik embodies a spirit of sharing and conviviality, whether through his contributions at conferences or in the kitchen.
