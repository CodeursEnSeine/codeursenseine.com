With over 15 years of experience, Ivan Dalmet is a seasoned React developer and UX design leader passionate about creating user-first digital experiences. He bridges the gap between design and development, crafting seamless, ergonomic interfaces that prioritize accessibility and efficiency. Drawing on his deep expertise, Ivan champions thoughtful workflows and a design-first mindset, enabling teams to deliver high-quality products with exceptional user experiences. His interests include web ergonomics, frontend performance, and fostering collaboration between design and development disciplines.

Co-founder and Lead Designer UI/UX of BearStudio.
Co-founder and organizer of Fork It! conferences.
