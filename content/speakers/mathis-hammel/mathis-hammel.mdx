Mathis Hammel is a tech evangelist at CodinGame, a website specialized in
mini-games to learn programming.

He is a specialist and technical advisor in cybersecurity, machine learning, and
algorithms. Mathis is passionate about technical challenges such as programming
competitions and Capture The Flag, and holds several titles from national and
international championships.
